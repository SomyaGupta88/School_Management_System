OPBMS excels in CBSE Science Exhibition
Harnoor Singh, class (X) student of Om Parkash Bansal Modern School, Mandi Gobindgarh bagged First Position in CBSE Regional Science exhibition held at Ludhiana on January 28 and 29,2019.
